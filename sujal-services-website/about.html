Our Story

Founded in 2013 by Anil Mohite, Sujal Services & Solutions began as a small-scale bearing supplier in Mumbai. Today, we serve industries across India with reliable bearing solutions.

What started as a passion for mechanical components has grown into a trusted business partnership with manufacturers, maintenance teams, and distributors nationwide.
